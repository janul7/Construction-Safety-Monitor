# Annotation Guidelines

## Classes

- person
- helmet
- vest

## Person

Annotate every clearly visible real worker.

## Helmet

Annotate only helmets being worn by a worker.
Do not annotate helmets lying on the ground.

## Vest

Annotate only visible high-visibility vests being worn by a worker.

## Occlusion

If a worker is partially visible, annotate the visible worker box.
Do not hallucinate hidden PPE.

## Ignore

- posters
- mannequins
- reflections
- printed people on signs
